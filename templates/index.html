How to Play:

Players take turns clicking on the moles as they appear to whack them! The game gets harder as time passes. Try to reach the target score before time runs out!

Mole Types:

  • Normal Mole: 1 point
  • Fast Mole (Red): 2 points
  • Golden Mole: 5 points

Power-ups:

  • Hammer (Red): Increases mole point value by 1 for 3 seconds
  • Freeze (Blue): Slows down mole disappearance for 3 seconds

Target Scores:

  • Easy: 30 points
  • Medium: 50 points
  • Hard: 80 points
